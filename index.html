Welcome to the website for BRAIN - MIND - COGNITIVE science journal club!

This is a Student-run Journal Club that focuses on Cognitive Science. We are interested in the mental representation of the human mind and how cognition is realized in the brain. We hold regular journal club meetings to talk about some exciting findings, cutting-edge research methods, or ground-breaking theories around brain, mind and cognition. We highly welcome students from different backgrounds to join in this journal club. If you are interested, please contact Eric Wang at eric.wang2004nz@link.cuhk.edu.hk

Meetings will be held roughly every 2 weeks. To participate, use the following Zoom [Link]
